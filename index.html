Decimal to DMS Converter

About This Tool

This tool is designed to make your life easier by converting decimal coordinates into Degrees, Minutes, Seconds (DMS) format with the correct cardinal directions (N, S, E, W). It's especially handy when copying coordinates from Google Maps and quickly needing them in DMS format without extra steps.

All you have to do is paste your decimal coordinates (latitude and longitude pair), and this tool will do the heavy lifting, outputting the DMS format that you can copy with just a click.

This tool was created because constantly splitting strings and handling separate fields for latitude and longitude was tedious. Now, everything is streamlined for quick use.

How It Works

Latitude and longitude are converted into DMS format, and you'll see N for positive latitudes or S for negative latitudes, and E or W for longitudes.
